Liszt: The Romantic Era Legend


Short Bio

Franz Liszt (1811-1886), born to a humble family with his father being a well-respected amateur musician. He would eventually be one of the most influencial composers and pianists within the classical field of music. He was regarded as the bridge in reviving the 18th century traditions, leading the 19th century and lastly a pioneer towards 20th century classical music.
